Follow smart money on Solana prediction markets. Mimic lets you automatically copy the trades of successful predictors.

## How It Works

1. **Discover** — Browse top predictors on Polymarket and other Solana prediction platforms
2. **Analyze** — See their win rate, ROI, and trading patterns
3. **Follow** — Set a follow amount and automatically mirror their positions
4. **Profit** — Ride the wisdom of winning traders

## Features

- Real-time trade copying
- Customizable follow amounts per trader
- Performance analytics dashboard
- Risk management controls (max position, stop-loss)
